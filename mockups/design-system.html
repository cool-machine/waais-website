Usage rules

Design decisions to carry into every page.

Do

  • Use water blue `#256F8F` for the navbar/top shell with white text.
  • Use dark dashboard/admin surfaces for focused, repeated workflows.
  • Use light homepage sections after the hero for readability and contrast.
  • Keep cards at 8px radius and avoid decorative card nesting.

Avoid

  • Do not make the whole public site one continuous dark block.
  • Do not overuse crimson for decorative elements; reserve it for emphasis and action.
  • Do not design around a final logo shape until George supplies the brand asset.
  • Do not put production Vue or Laravel code in the mockups folder.
